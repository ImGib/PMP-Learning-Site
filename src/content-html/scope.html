Scope trả lời câu hỏi "dự án sẽ làm những gì (và KHÔNG làm gì)?". Kiểm soát scope kém là nguyên nhân thất bại số 1.

ENProduct Scope — the features and functions that characterize a product, service, or result. (tính năng & chức năng của sản phẩm)

Hai "kẻ thù" của Scope

ENScope Creep — the uncontrolled expansion to product or project scope without adjustments to time, cost, and resources. (phạm vi phình ra không qua change control)
ENGold Plating — adding extra features or work not requested by the customer, beyond the agreed requirements. (team tự thêm tính năng thừa)
Scope CreepPhạm vi phình ra không kiểm soát — khách/team thêm việc mà không qua change control. "Creep" = bò lan.
Gold PlatingTeam tự ý thêm tính năng khách không yêu cầu ("mạ vàng"). Lãng phí, không tạo value.
Mẹo ghi nhớ · Creep vs Gold PlatingScope Creep = khách/nhiều bên đẩy vào (thay đổi lén, không kiểm soát). Gold Plating = TEAM tự thêm (tưởng tốt nhưng thừa). Cả hai đều xấu — luôn bám change control.

WBS — Work Breakdown Structure

WBS vs Activity vs MilestoneWBS (thuộc Scope) phân rã tới Work Package — trả lời "LÀM GÌ". Activity (thuộc Schedule, sinh ra ở Define Activities) là việc nhỏ hơn để thực thi work package — trả lời "làm THẾ NÀO / KHI NÀO". Milestone = mốc quan trọng, duration = 0 (vd "Module A xong", "ký hợp đồng").
ENWork Breakdown Structure (WBS) — a hierarchical decomposition of the total scope of work to be carried out by the project team to accomplish the project objectives and create the required deliverables. (cây phân rã công việc)
ENWork Package — the work defined at the lowest level of the WBS for which cost and duration can be estimated and managed. (phần tử thấp nhất của WBS)
ENScope Baseline — the approved version of the scope statement, WBS, and its associated WBS dictionary, changed only through formal change control. (Scope Statement + WBS + WBS Dictionary)

WBS = "cây phân rã công việc": chia nhỏ toàn bộ scope thành các phần quản lý được. WBS nói CÁI GÌ (không nói how/when). Phần tử thấp nhất của WBS gọi là Work Package.

  • Scope Baseline = 3 thành phần: Scope Statement + WBS + WBS Dictionary (từ điển mô tả chi tiết từng phần).
  • Rule 100%: tổng công việc các "con" = 100% của "cha"; không dư, không thiếu.
  • Rule 8/80: một work package nên tốn từ 8 đến 80 giờ (không quá nhỏ, không quá lớn).
  • RTM (Requirements Traceability Matrix) — ma trận truy vết: nối mỗi yêu cầu với mục tiêu & deliverable, theo dõi suốt vòng đời.
Validate Scope ≠ Control QualityControl Quality → tạo Verified deliverables (đội QC kiểm đúng/sai — nội bộ). Validate Scope → tạo Accepted deliverables (khách/sponsor ký nghiệm thu — chính thức). Trình tự: kiểm chất lượng trước, khách nghiệm thu sau.
Mẹo ghi nhớ · Verified vs AcceptedVerified = "đúng chưa?" (nội bộ QC). Accepted = "khách gật đầu chưa?" (ký nhận). Còn MoSCoW để ưu tiên yêu cầu: Must · Should · Could · Won't-this-time.

Scope's two "enemies"

ENScope Creep — the uncontrolled expansion to product or project scope without adjustments to time, cost, and resources.
ENGold Plating — adding extra features or work not requested by the customer, beyond the agreed requirements.
Scope CreepUncontrolled growth — the customer/team adds work without going through change control. "Creep" = it slowly spreads.
Gold PlatingThe team adds features the customer did not ask for ("gold plating"). Wasteful, adds no value.
Memory tip · Creep vs Gold PlatingScope Creep = pushed in by others (sneaky, uncontrolled change). Gold Plating = the TEAM adds it (well-meant but unnecessary). Both are bad — always stick to change control.

WBS — Work Breakdown Structure

WBS vs Activity vs MilestoneWBS (part of Scope) decomposes down to the Work Package — answers "WHAT". An Activity (part of Schedule, created in Define Activities) is a smaller task to execute a work package — answers "HOW / WHEN". A Milestone = a key marker with duration = 0 (e.g. "Module A done", "contract signed").
ENWork Breakdown Structure (WBS) — a hierarchical decomposition of the total scope of work to be carried out by the project team to accomplish the project objectives and create the required deliverables.
ENWork Package — the work defined at the lowest level of the WBS for which cost and duration can be estimated and managed.
ENScope Baseline — the approved version of the scope statement, WBS, and its associated WBS dictionary, changed only through formal change control.

WBS = the "work decomposition tree": break the whole scope into manageable pieces. The WBS says WHAT (not how/when). The lowest element of the WBS is called a Work Package.

  • Scope Baseline = 3 components: Scope Statement + WBS + WBS Dictionary (which describes each element in detail).
  • 100% Rule: the sum of the "children" = 100% of the "parent"; nothing extra, nothing missing.
  • 8/80 Rule: a work package should take 8 to 80 hours (not too small, not too large).
  • RTM (Requirements Traceability Matrix): links each requirement to its objective & deliverable, tracked across the whole life cycle.
Validate Scope ≠ Control QualityControl Quality → produces Verified deliverables (QC checks correct/incorrect — internal). Validate Scope → produces Accepted deliverables (customer/sponsor sign off — formal). Order: check quality first, customer accepts after.
Memory tip · Verified vs AcceptedVerified = "is it correct?" (internal QC). Accepted = "did the customer sign off?" Also MoSCoW to prioritise requirements: Must · Should · Could · Won't-this-time.
